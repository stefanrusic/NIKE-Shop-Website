What is NIKE mission?

Our mission is:

What drives us to do everything possible to expand human potential. We do that by creating groundbreaking sport innovations, by making our products more sustainably, by building a creative and diverse global team and by making a positive impact in communities where we live and work.

Countless ideas are tested in pursuit of aiding performance, injury risk reduction, enhancing perception and feel, and delivery of innovative products to athletes. To bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete* in the world.
*If you have a body, you are an athlete.

To learn more about how we deliver on that mission, please visit About Nike.
